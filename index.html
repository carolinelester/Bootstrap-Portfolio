My Bio:

My name’s Caroline but my friends all call me Lester! I’m an Industrial Engineer hailing from the deep south, USA currently working in Aerospace and private jets. I’m super passionate about international travel and culture, espresso, art and house-techno music. I recently began a web development and coding journey by seeking a professional certificate from University of California, Los Angeles. I’m also learning Adobe Creative and graphic design principles. I plan to specialize in front end web development and design. I’ve decided to begin a career change because I feel that the traditional engineering role I currently am in inhibits my natural creativity. I love that web development is the perfect compromise between art and engineering. I’d like to combine my previous experience with my unique artistic identity to create a beautiful online experience for all.
